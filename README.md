# Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents a Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance across products, regions, and time. It provides a clear view of trends, product performance, and overall business insights.

## Objective
To understand sales patterns, identify top and low-performing products, and support better decision-making.

## Key Insights
- Classic Cars contribute the highest sales among product lines  
- Sales peaked in 2004 and dropped significantly in 2005  
- Few products drive most of the revenue (top-heavy distribution)  
- Some products consistently underperform and need attention  

## Features
- Sales breakdown by product line and product name  
- Year-wise sales trend analysis  
- Top 5 and Bottom 5 product comparison  
- Quantity ordered analysis across categories  
- Interactive visuals for better exploration  

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Data Visualization  

## Outcome
The dashboard helps identify high-performing areas, detect weak spots, and improve strategic planning for sales and operations.
